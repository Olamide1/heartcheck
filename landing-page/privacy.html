Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Information We Collect

HeartCheck collects minimal personal information necessary to provide our relationship wellness services:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and relationship start date
  • Check-in Data: Daily mood and connection ratings, reflections, and sharing preferences
  • Usage Data: App interactions and feature usage to improve our service
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for technical support

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized relationship insights and recommendations
  • Generate pattern alerts and exercise suggestions
  • Send daily check-in reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve our app features and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data at any time through the app settings. You can also opt out of notifications and data sharing with your partner.

Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@heartcheck-couples.com
